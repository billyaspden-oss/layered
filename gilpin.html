01 / The Brief

Paper-based training for a five-star hotel wasn't working.

The Gilpin Hotel & Lake House is one of England's most celebrated independent hotels — a Relais & Châteaux property with two distinct venues, four restaurant concepts, a spa, and a team spanning 13 departments across both properties.

Staff training was largely paper-based and inconsistent. SOPs lived in binders. Institutional knowledge walked out the door when staff moved on. Operational reporting was manual, maintenance tracking was scattered, and there was no central system connecting training, operations, and management.

The brief evolved from a simple digital training hub into something far more ambitious: a full operations platform that could handle AI-powered staff training, live maintenance and issue management, shift handovers, KPI tracking, inventory management, and automated reporting — all in one system, accessible from any device.

And at its core: an AI assistant that could answer any operational question in real time, drawing on Gilpin's own knowledge base.

02 / The AI System

Meet Plato — Gilpin's AI operations assistant.

Plato is a custom AI assistant built specifically for the Gilpin team. Powered by Anthropic Claude with semantic search across SOPs, training videos, and operations logs, it answers any operational question a staff member has — instantly and accurately, grounded in Gilpin's own documentation.

The staff platform

"What's the allergen information for tonight's amuse-bouche?"
"What's our dog policy at Lake House?"
"How do I process a late checkout?"

Questions that used to mean tracking down a manager, hunting through a binder, or guessing — Plato answers in seconds, with accuracy grounded in Gilpin's own documents. It knows when to escalate to a senior team member and has built-in safety escalation for safeguarding and wellbeing concerns.

Live operations

Not just training — a full operations layer.

Beyond training, Plato handles day-to-day hotel operations. Staff can log maintenance issues, report guest complaints, record accidents, track lost property, book taxis, and manage shift handovers — all flowing into Knack for live tracking with full audit trails and automated notifications.

The management layer

Prof Plato — the system that runs behind the team.

Prof Plato is a separate management application for heads of department. It has its own AI assistant with 20+ Claude tool-use functions — it can read, write, and format Google Docs, search operations logs, query KPI data, and generate AI avatar training videos. SOPs authored in Prof Plato flow straight into Plato's knowledge base.

03 / What We Built

Two applications. One codebase. ~24,000 lines of Python.

AI Chat & Operations

Claude-powered AI assistant with semantic search across SOPs, training videos, and operations logs. Handles training questions, maintenance logging, guest issues, accident reporting, lost property, and stores queries — each with its own specialised conversation mode.

SOP Library & Governance

Structured, searchable SOP library with trust scoring (0-100), staleness alerts, HOD review workflows, sign-off tracking, and reliability tiers. Department-scoped Google Drive integration with content-SHA verified edits.

Maintenance & Estates

Kanban maintenance board with job timers, sign-off workflows, pre-planned maintenance scheduling with recurring rules, and Estates KPI dashboards calculated from KeyPay payroll data. Similar-job time estimation via semantic retrieval.

Quizzes & Gamification

Auto-generated quizzes from SOPs via Claude Haiku, spaced repetition scheduling, placement tests, four-tier progression, XP system, streaks with freeze support, 40+ badges, daily department challenges, and team streak tracking.

Video Pipeline

Three video paths: Google Drive ingest with Whisper transcription and hallucination detection, SOP-to-video generation with branded slides and TTS narration (ElevenLabs with Google Cloud fallback), and Synthesia AI avatar videos.

Prof Plato Admin

Separate management application with its own AI assistant (20+ Claude tool-use functions), document management with Google Drive/Docs, operations log analysis with anomaly detection, automated daily briefing emails, and staff provisioning from payroll.

04 / Design Direction

Luxury-grade design for the people who deliver the luxury.

The Gilpin brand is built on warmth, quality, and understated elegance. The staff portal needed to feel like a natural extension of that — not a generic HR software tool bolted onto their world.

The dark theme with a dot-pattern background creates a sense of depth and quality. The Gilpin gold — #C46A3A — is used sparingly as an accent throughout. Playfair Display italic in the hero echoes the serif typefaces used across the Gilpin marketing site.

Real hotel photography is used throughout — the hero pulls directly from the Gilpin marketing site, so it always reflects the property as it actually looks.

Plato and Prof Plato are live across the Gilpin Hotel and Lake House. Staff use it daily — for training, for operations logging, for looking up procedures mid-service. New starters onboard through placement tests and structured tier progression. Maintenance teams track jobs with timers and sign-offs.
